Hi, I'm Addison

I'm a Full Stack Web Developer living in Houston, TX. I'm originally from South Florida and attended Rice University for my undergraduate degree where I majored in Health Sciences & Film Studies. After graduating, I began teaching Algebra at a Charter School in North Houston. While I found the work important and rewarding, I wanted something more technically challenging and decided to transition into web development.


While working as a Web Specialist for the School of Social Sciences at Rice University, I had to learn a lot about coding through trial and error. I wanted to hone my skills as a coder, so I decided to enroll in DigitalCraft's six month, part-time flex coding bootcamp, which I graduated in April of 2019. During my time in the course I discovered my passion for front-end design and affinity for working with react and redux.

Now On View

Curatorial site where users can discover works of art in the Metropolitan Museum of Art's collection. Search through artworks in the collection by exploring different tags and select images to save them to your gallery. Zoom in on each artwork in your gallery to learn additional information and select images to sort them into exhibitions. Organize your exhibitions however you choose by positioning artworks in the workspace.

  • Created using HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, Redux, and Axios.
  • Used the Metropolitan Museum of Art Collection API for artwork data.

Small Potatoes

Trivia game application where users can select a category and level of difficulty and play a 10 question round of trivia. "My Stats" page keeps track of your best score & time, as well as your average score and total time played. "Pick Your Toppings" to change the site background.

  • Created using HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, Redux, and Axios.
  • Used the Open Trivia Database API for question data and the GIPHY API for randomized background styling.

Ace Adoption

E-commerce site where users can adopt a new best friend. Create an account to get started and login to your profile. Search through different breeds, genders, price ranges, locations and ages to find the dog that best fits you. Add dogs to your cart to finalize the adoption.

  • Group project created using HTML, CSS, Javascript, Express, Node.js, Passport.js, PostgresQL, and Sequelize.
