Hi, I'm Addison

I'm a Full Stack Web Developer living in Houston, TX. I'm originally from South Florida and attended Rice University for my undergraduate degree where I majored in Health Sciences & Film Studies. After graduating, I began teaching Algebra at a Charter School in North Houston. While I found the work important and rewarding, I wanted something more technically challenging and decided to transition into web development.


While working as a Web Specialist for the School of Social Sciences at Rice University, I had to learn a lot about coding through trial and error. I wanted to hone my skills as a coder, so I decided to enroll in DigitalCraft's six month, part-time flex coding bootcamp, which I graduated in April of 2019. During my time in the course I discovered my passion for front-end design and affinity for working with react and redux.
